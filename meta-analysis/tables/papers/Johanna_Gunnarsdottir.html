Group C: Exposure & Control Definition
RM thresholdDose-response: 0, 1, 2, and ≥3 prior miscarriages (no single RM threshold)
Type of lossesSelf-reported spontaneous pregnancy losses, regardless of gestational age or etiology; not limited to consecutive losses
Gestational age cutoffNot specified; self-reported; no gestational age cutoff for the loss stated
Dose-responseYes — four tiers: 0 (reference), 1, 2, and ≥3 prior miscarriages
Control groupPrimiparous women with no prior miscarriage (n = 536,169), same national register, same period
Group D: Outcomes & Methods
Outcomes measuredPreeclampsia (overall, preterm, term); stillbirth (≥28 wks, overall, preterm, term); SGA (>2 SD below mean, overall, preterm, term); placental abruption (overall, preterm, term); spontaneous preterm birth <37 wks (overall, very preterm <32 wks, moderately preterm 32–36 wks)
Effect measureOR (odds ratio)
Statistical methodUnconditional logistic regression; no prior miscarriage = reference
Confounders adjustedMaternal age, early pregnancy BMI, height, smoking, country of birth, years of formal education, IVF, chronic hypertension, pregestational diabetes, hypothyroidism, SLE, fetal sex, year of birth

Group F: Quality Notes
Data source qualityHigh — national registry >98% birth coverage; ICD codes validated; gestational age by ultrasound in 97%
Confounder adjustmentYes — extensive: age, BMI, height, smoking, country of birth, education, IVF, chronic HTN, pregestational DM, hypothyroidism, SLE, fetal sex, year of birth. Not adjusted for thrombophilia or PCOS (acknowledged limitation).
Outcome validationICD-9 and ICD-10 codes from national registry; preeclampsia diagnosis previously validated (93% of coded cases confirmed)
Bias acknowledgedSelf-reported miscarriage data (no gestational age or etiology); no data on induced abortions; no adjustment for thrombophilia or PCOS; effect measure modification by age tested and found non-significant
Other notesLargest study in the collection (N > 619,000); restricted to primiparous women to avoid parity confounding; dose-response analysis is a major strength; no single RM threshold. Women with 2 mc show intermediate risk; ≥3 mc show strongest associations especially for preterm disorders.
Abbreviations: RM = recurrent miscarriage; mc = miscarriage; OR = odds ratio; aOR = adjusted odds ratio; CI = confidence interval; NR = not reported; PTB = preterm birth; PE = preeclampsia; SGA = small for gestational age; IVF = in vitro fertilization; BMI = body mass index; HTN = hypertension; DM = diabetes mellitus; SLE = systemic lupus erythematosus; PCOS = polycystic ovary syndrome; ICD = International Classification of Diseases.
